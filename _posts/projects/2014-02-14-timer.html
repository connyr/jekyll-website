What can it do?
Stopwatch, timer and alarm functionality

Technologies and Classes involved:
NSTimer, Concurrency, UILocalNotification, NSTimeInterval, UIDatePicker

Description

A countdown timer, similar in appearance and functionality to apples clock app.
Functionalities: Converting between decimal and fraction representation, GCD(euclid's greatest common divisor), simplifying, general operations between fractions (+,-,*,/)
