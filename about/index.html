Some Details

Education

My full name is Cornelia Rehbein. Born and raised in Frankfurt Oder, Germany, I graduated from the Hasso Plattner Institut with a B.Sc. degree and am now working towards a M.Sc. degree in IT Systems Engineering. My resume is available on LinkedIn.

Experience

I've worked as a research assistant on projects involving 3D Point Clouds, 3D Point Cloud Analysis and 3D Model Generalization at the institute. Between 2011 and 2012, I interned at SAP Labs in Palo Alto, California for a year where I developed applications for mobile devices.

Development

In general, I am more interested in front-end development than backend, but I experienced both sites. I currently enjoy building mobile applications and user-facing web apps. Recently, I've also become interested in natural language processing. Most of my time is spent on personal projects, on github and on reddit and twitter.
